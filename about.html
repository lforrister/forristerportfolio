Lindsay Forrister

Hi, I'm Lindsay.

I'm a Front End Web Developer based in Dallas, TX, with a background in graphic design and a passion for creating exceptional user experiences.

In my current role as Front End Developer at Lifeblue, a digital agency, I help bring our client’s visions to life through thoughtful development solutions and beautiful, responsive styles. I work with brands including AT&T, Heifer International, The Dallas Zoo, Perennials Fabrics, FC Dallas, and many more. I am also involved with the company’s initial innovative product team, in which we create SaaS products to aid cultural institutions, sporting events, and other programs. Additionally, I have been able to contribute to Lifeblue’s own front end development framework - Motif.

Prior to my time at Lifeblue, I enjoyed my role as Web Designer at Medieval Times. While there, I contributed to their main website, assisted in an overhaul of their ticketing UX/UI, and aided in developing a new ‘Partner Portal’ dashboard. Additionally, I aided in their marketing and design departments through graphic design, video editing, email design & development, and on-site production assistance.

Before venturing into the web development world, I started off my career at Core Publishing as an art director, in which I was responsible for the art direction of covers, article layouts, and promotional materials for 3 of their publications: Dallas Hotel Magazine, Houston Hotel Magazine, & Colorado Hotel Magazine. Along with my art direction duties, I also played a large hand in scheduling and production management of each of these publications.
