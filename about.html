Lindsay Forrister

Hi, I'm Lindsay.

I graduated from Southern Methodist University in May 2016 with a Bachelor of Arts in Advertising (Creative Track), a Bachelor of Arts in Music (Voice), and a minor in graphic design. Post grad, I began working as an Associate Art Director at Core Publishing, which produces Dallas Hotel Magazine, Houston Hotel Magazine, Denver Hotel Magazine and OC InRoom Magazine.

In April 2017, I began Southern Methodist University's Coding Bootcamp - a 6 month, full-stack web development program. Technologies learned include HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, JavaScript, jQuery, APIs, AJAX, JSON, node.js, Firebase, MySQL, Express, and Handlebars, as well as UX/UI programs such as Sketch and InVision.

I am currently a Web Designer at Medieval Times, where I assist in updating our website, provide front end development and design for ongoing projects, such as our ticketing API, aid in designing/developing our email marketing campaigns, and assist in production materials. In addition to my previous skill set, in this position I have also learned new languages and skills such as SASS, PHP, Laravel, and Adobe Animate.

Whether it be through front end web development, UX/UI design, or graphic design, I am passionate about creating a high quality experience, both in aesthetic and functionality. I hope I can share that passion with you!

Thanks so much for stopping by, and feel free to get in touch - I'd love to hear from you!
