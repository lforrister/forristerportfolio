Manifesto

From your scrapes on the playground to your blisters from your new cleates, Band Aid has always been there to protect your skin. But it's not about protecting your skin - Band Aid belives in the power of embracing the skin you're in. So whatever gender you identify with, Band Aid encourages you to do what you love regardless of traditional gender stereotypes. You know we'll always be there to protect the skin you're in - with BandAid, you're fully covered.
