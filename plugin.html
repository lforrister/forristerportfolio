About

Plug in is a web application that allows users to search for music events in their area using the Eventful API.

My Role

In this project, I was responsible for the UX/UI design and front end development of the project through the use of HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, Javascript & jQuery. I was also responsible for implementing the Google Maps API, which connected to our Firebase database in order to retrieve the user's selected information.

Future Ideas

Ideas to expand this project include: allowing users to log in through social media accounts in order to connect with their current friends who are interested in attending their events, or to suggest new friends to attend the event with; implementing a Spotify API that would allow users to listen to a playlist of the artist they are going to see, as well as the opening artist they may not be as familiar with.
